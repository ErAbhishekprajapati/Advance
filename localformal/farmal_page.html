Frequently Asked Questions

We charge a 15% commission on each sale, which includes payment processing, customer support, and marketing. There are no upfront fees or subscription costs.

Payments are processed weekly. You'll receive your earnings every Tuesday for the previous week's orders, minus our commission.

While we welcome certified organic farms, certification is not required. We do require transparent farming practices - you'll be able to describe your methods on your farm profile.

You have options: you can use our logistics partners (additional fee applies), handle delivery yourself, or offer farm pickup. You set your delivery areas and schedules in your farm settings.
